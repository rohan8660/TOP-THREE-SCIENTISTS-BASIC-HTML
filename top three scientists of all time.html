TOP THREE GREAT SCIENTISTS

1. SIR ISSAC NEWTON

Sir Isaac Newton , 25 December 1642 - 20 March 1726 , was an English physicist, mathematician and astronomer.He is famous for his work on the laws of motion, optics, gravity, and calculus.

2. ALFRED NOBEL

Alfred Nobel (October 21, 1833 - December 10, 1896) was a Swedish scientist, engineer, and weapons manufacturer. He is well known for the invention of dynamite and for creating the Nobel Prize.

3. ALBERT EINSTEIN

Albert Einstein (14 March 1879 - 18 April 1955) was a German-born theoretical physicist who developed the general theory of relativity, one of the two pillars of modern physics (alongside quantum mechanics).
